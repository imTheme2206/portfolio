Type: AFK

## What to build

Present one primary email action, current availability, essential professional links, and deliberate CV or resume downloads. - [ ] Empty or unsupported social destinations are removed.
- [ ] The contact section works in normal flow and does not depend on negative stacking or sentinel state to become usable.
