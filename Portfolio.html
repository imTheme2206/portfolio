01 — ABOUT

A frontend engineer
with a designer's obsession.

I build soft, considered interfaces — the kind that feel less like software and more like a well-worn book. Specialising in motion, micro-typography and the unglamorous craft of making things feel right.

Seven years in, I've shipped product across fintech, editorial, and creative tooling — usually as the lone engineer wedged between a design team and a backend that doesn't yet exist. I like that seam.

My work tends to be quiet on the surface and busy underneath: hand-tuned springs, GPU-friendly transitions, accessibility worked in from the first commit. I care about the 50ms most people never notice.

When I'm not at the keyboard you'll find me developing film in a converted bathroom, or losing at chess in a café in Alfama.
