Type: AFK

## What to build

Connect career achievements and working principles to relevant project proof, then use the portrait and personal copy to explain collaboration style, preferred problems, and future direction.

- [ ] Relevant achievements link to available case studies or Lab artifacts.
- [ ] The `300vh` experience introduction and `200%` About pin are removed.
- [ ] Mobile renders all content in normal flow with no fixed-height copy containers.
